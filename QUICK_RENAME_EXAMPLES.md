## ‚úÖ **Name Guidelines**

### **Recommended:**
- **Length**: 8-15 characters
- **Characters**: Letters, numbers, spaces, hyphens, apostrophes
- **Style**: Professional, memorable, descriptive

### **Avoid:**
- **Too long**: May get truncated
- **Special characters**: Stick to basics
- **Very generic**: "App" alone is not descriptive
